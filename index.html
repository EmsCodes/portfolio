The developer

Hello World! I'm Endre, a 31 year old Front-end developer from northern Norway.

I lost my previous job as a a legal-secretary due to covid. This led to a career-change into front-end developing, and I just finished a higher professional degree in front-end development at Noroff - School of technology and digital media.

As a developer I love to create good looking and user-friendly sites. I'm constantly trying to improve my technical and design skills. I have learned a lot in 2 years, but there are of course still a lot for me to learn.

My goal is to find a company to work for where I can specialise in a one or more skills, and also improve as a frontend-developer in general.
